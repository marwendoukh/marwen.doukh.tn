During 2016 we've seen many great products that changed our life and also there are a lot of news happened that affected the tech world like the US election. There is always something new to come in the tech world , and obviously 2017 will not be an exception. In this blog post , I'll tell you, briefly, my expectations for this year 2017.

HTTPS

In 2016, we've experienced a big increase in the HTTPS adoption by many websites .And this become a must-have for every website developer because user privacy is mandatory nowadays. Mozilla and many other big companies have sponsored a project called let's encrypt which offers free TLS certificates for everyone.Recently, Mozilla announced that more than 50% of web pages visited by Firefox users were secured by HTTPS . This is a very important step toward a secure web .I expect that 2017 will be the year of HTTPS and I hope that more and more websites will be secured with HTTPS.

Encrypted chat Apps

Whatsapp , the 1 Billion users chat App announced that it can't access the content of the messages sent by its users because they are end-to-end encrypted. Same for Viber , Telegram (secret chat) , and Signal . The Snowden-approved chat App Signal , announced that it has an increase in its App download especially after the US election. Using one of these Apps , you can privately connect to your friends and family without being watched by big companies , governments and hackers. I expect that 2017 will be the year in which we can connect securely to our friends.

Virtual Reality

With many projects like Aframe (a Mozilla library for creating VR scene using HTML) , I expect that in 2017 we can visit many countries , attend Mozilla , Google , Apple events without leaving home , playing games in VR scenes ...

Artificial Intelligence

Lately in 2016 , we heard a lot about IA assistants like Google Assistant and Jarvis which is an AI assistant announced by Mark Zuckerberg. In 2017, I expect that AI will be integrated in most of 2017's products and help us make things done.

2017 wishes

I wish that Firefox , will get more market share because , honestly, it deserves to be the first browser in the world. In this moment , Mozilla is working on many privacy features for Firefox in its Nightly version (Alpha version) as well as integrating Rust into its browser, this will make it way faster.

Let's work hard to make our future better and let's put one foot in front of another everyday !
